Who We Are

Adesa Charity Aid Foundation is a community-driven, non-governmental organization based in Mbale, Uganda. We serve orphans, support vulnerable families, and bring practical solutions to those most in need.

Our Impact

Orphanage Construction

We create safe, nurturing environments for orphaned children to grow, learn, and thrive.

Food Relief Programs

We deliver hot meals and nutritional support to underfed communities and school children.

Medical Outreach

We run free health camps providing checkups, medication, and health education.

Support Our Mission

Your support helps us build homes, deliver food, and save lives in Mbale and beyond.
